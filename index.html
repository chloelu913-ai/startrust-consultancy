🎓 How to Apply for a Singapore Student Pass

Guide by StarTrust Consultancy · Updated May 2026

What Is a Singapore Student Pass?

A Student Pass (STP) is issued by ICA and allows foreign students to study full-time at approved Singapore institutions.

Step-by-Step Application

  • Step 1: Secure a Letter of Acceptance from your chosen Singapore school
  • Step 2: School or student agent submits via SOLAR system
  • Step 3: ICA processes in 2–4 weeks and issues IPA letter
  • Step 4: Travel to Singapore with IPA, passport, and documents
  • Step 5: Report to ICA within 7 days to collect Student Pass card

Documents Required

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Letter of Acceptance from school
  • Recent passport-size photos
  • Academic certificates and transcripts
  • Proof of financial ability (bank statements)

Tips for Success

  • Start at least 2–3 months before course begins
  • Ensure all documents are in English
  • Use a registered student agent for SOLAR submission

📚 Polytechnic vs Private Diploma in Singapore

Guide by StarTrust Consultancy · Updated May 2026

Singapore Polytechnics

  • Government-funded: Ngee Ann, SP, TP, RP, NP
  • 3-year diploma; fees ~S$3,500–S$4,500/yr for foreigners
  • Strong industry partnerships and internship placements
  • Competitive entry; O-Level or equivalent required
  • Direct articulation to local universities

Private Diploma Schools (PEIs)

  • TMC Academy — Business, IT, Psychology; affordable fees; flexible intakes (Jan, Apr, Jul, Oct); articulation to overseas degrees. StarTrust can assist with applications.
  • PSB Academy — Business and engineering; wide university network
  • MDIS — Business, fashion, nursing, engineering degrees
  • Kaplan Singapore — CPA pathways, business, IT
  • James Cook University Singapore — Full Australian university campus
  • EASB — Hospitality, business, travel; popular with international students

Fees and Flexibility

  • PEI fees: ~S$8,000–S$22,000/year depending on school and course
  • More flexible intake dates than polytechnics
  • Faster pathways to degree via overseas university partnerships

Which Is Better?

  • Strong O-Level results + lower cost → Polytechnic
  • Flexible entry + direct degree pathway → Private PEI
  • Both offer routes to university degrees
